About Us

Brief Description

The Financial Decision Company for Collective Finance aims to be the link between landowners, real estate developers, and investors, through the introduction of a decision platform to put forward debt instruments to finance real estate developers’ projects by directing financing to purchase the project’s asset (land) and enabling investors to invest in it by using technical solutions in legitimate and safe ways and obtaining securities that preserve their rights (the instruments).
