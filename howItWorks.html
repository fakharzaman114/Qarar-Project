How It Works

Step by Step

Qarar platform directly connects creditworthy companies looking for project financing with investors looking to support their growth.

How to become an Investor How to become a Borrower

How to become Investor

Invest With Us

Registration

The investor must register on the platform by following the registration steps and filling in the required information

Open and fund an investment portfolio account

An investment portfolio is created after activating the account on the platform by following the account activation steps. This enables the investor to easily and conveniently fund his virtual investment account.

Find the right project

Among many opportunities and projects, search for the best and most suitable one after sufficient study in detail

Project Selection

Investing in the appropriate amount estimated by the investor as a minimum of 1,000 riyals against the number of sukuk to be invested in

Own Murabaha Sukuk and receive returns

Shareholders own Murabaha Sukuk and the returns are distributed and the Sukuk are redeemed on the date agreed upon in the issuance prospectus.

How to become Borrower

Real Estate Developer

Registration

Register as an entrepreneur to ensure the standards of your project in accordance with the financing terms of the Mudarabah platform

Apply for financing

Submit a financing application, fill out the requirements and attach supporting documents via the platform

Review the application and evaluate the investment opportunity.

Communicate with the real estate developer by the platform management to ensure the feasibility of the investment opportunity and the required financing amount.

Post the opportunity on the platform and raise funds.

The opportunity will be ready and will be displayed on the platform for investors to purchase the Sukuk.

Own the real estate and start the development project

The developer owns the real estate asset and begins work on the development project.

Distribution of proceeds and extinguishment of sukuk

The returns are distributed to investors on the specified maturity date in accordance with the approved prospectus and the amortization of the Sukuk.
