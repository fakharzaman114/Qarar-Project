About Us

Brief Description

Qarar Financial Company aims to be the link between land owners, real estate developers, and investors in the field of real estate development, by financing real estate developers’ projects by directing financing to purchase the project asset (land) and enabling investors to invest in it using financial technology solutions in legitimate and safe ways.

This is done by offering real estate investment opportunities on the Qarar platform and collecting funds from investors through financial technology solutions.

Why Qarar?

The financing of collective ownership is a major supporter of Vision 2030, as it contributes to the creation of new channels for financing and investment targeting various sectors, and contributes to achieving economic depth, adds value to GDP, and encourages the creation of economic entities and new job opportunities and the achievement of several economic and social benefits by supporting the financing of various Projects. In the financial decision platform, we enable individuals and companies to invest in real estate development with amounts starting from 1000 riyals, and we enable real estate developers to obtain appropriate financing through financing solutions compatible with Islamic law to develop their business and transfer them to a higher stage of service and competition.
